# Essential Pages Specification

## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: FAQ page functionality
The system SHALL provide a comprehensive FAQ page.

#### Scenario: User has questions about services
- Given the user navigates to the FAQ page
- When the page loads
- Then common questions SHALL be displayed
- And answers SHALL be clear and informative
- And questions SHALL be organized by category
- And search or filter functionality SHALL be available

### Requirement: Gallery page with before/after photos
The system SHALL display a professional gallery of treatment results.

#### Scenario: User wants to see treatment results
- Given the user navigates to the gallery page
- When the page loads
- Then before/after photos SHALL be displayed
- And images SHALL be high quality and professional
- And photos SHALL be organized by treatment type
- And viewing experience SHALL be impressive

### Requirement: About us page
The system SHALL provide information about the clinic and team.

#### Scenario: User wants to learn about the clinic
- Given the user visits the about page
- When the page loads
- Then clinic information SHALL be displayed
- And team credentials SHALL be shown
- And facility information SHALL be provided
- And trust-building content SHALL be included

### Requirement: Contact page with location
The system SHALL provide comprehensive contact information.

#### Scenario: User needs to contact or visit the clinic
- Given the user navigates to the contact page
- When the page loads
- Then address and map SHALL be displayed
- And phone number and email SHALL be visible
- And business hours SHALL be clearly shown
- And contact form SHALL be available

### Requirement: Legal pages (Terms & Privacy)
The system SHALL include necessary legal information pages.

#### Scenario: User needs to review legal information
- Given the user accesses legal pages
- When the pages load
- Then terms of service SHALL be displayed
- And privacy policy SHALL be available
- And content SHALL be professional and comprehensive
- And pages SHALL be easily accessible from footer
